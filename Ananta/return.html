#RETURN & EXCHANGE POLICY.

RETURN POLICY

Eligible returns of products purchased on Ananta.com may be made by mail or in store. Items must be returned in accordance with the requirements outlined below, within 30 days (10 days in the case of beauty products) from the date of delivery to the Ananta return address listed on the return shipping label or to a Ananta store in the United States (excluding Ananta outlet stores). Please note, corporate gifts must be returned exclusively to Ananta.com.

EXCHANGE POLICY

We are happy to facilitate exchanges for online purchases. To request an exchange, please contact our Client Advisors at +1.877.482.2430. Returned merchandise must meet the conditions and guidelines listed in the Online Return Policy in order to be accepted for an exchange. Please note exchanges are subject to merchandise availability.

REQUEST TRACKER

Items purchased online may be returned, in accordance with the Online Return Policy requirements, within 30 days (10 days in the case of beauty products) from the date of delivery, to any Gucci store in the United States (excluding Ananta outlet stores or department stores such as Nordstrom, Saks Fifth Avenue, Neiman Marcus, Bergdorf Goodman, etc.). To locate a store near you, please visit our store locator.

To facilitate your return, please bring a printed version of the digital invoice you received in your shipment confirmation email. Refunds will be submitted once the return has been received and processed at the warehouse.

Any original shipping charges incurred at the time of purchase are non-refundable.
